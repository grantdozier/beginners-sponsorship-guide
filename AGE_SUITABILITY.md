# Age Suitability — Beginners Sponsorship Guide

**Rating:** 18+
**Effective date:** 2026-04-18

This page explains why the *Beginners Sponsorship Guide* app is rated 18+ on
the App Store and what kind of content you will find in it.

---

## Who this app is for

This app is a companion for people working through the Twelve Steps of
Alcoholics Anonymous (A.A.), and for their sponsors. It digitizes a paper
workbook written by a member of A.A. to help new sponsors guide a sponsee
through Step Four ("Made a searching and fearless moral inventory of
ourselves").

It is not a social network. It is not a dating app. It is not a game. It
is a private journaling and worksheet tool designed to be used by adults in
recovery, typically in consultation with an experienced sponsor.

---

## Why the 18+ rating

The rating is driven by three elements:

### 1. Frequent and central discussion of alcohol and drug use

Recovery from alcoholism is the entire subject of this app. The guide
discusses, in detail:

- How alcohol affects the body ("physical allergy"), the mind ("mental
  obsession"), and the spirit ("spiritual malady")
- Personal drinking histories as examples for learning
- The relationship between drinking, relapse, and recovery

These references are frequent and central, not incidental.

### 2. Mature/suggestive themes in the Sex Conduct Inventory

Step Four of A.A. includes an inventory of past sexual conduct, from
chapter five of the book *Alcoholics Anonymous*. This inventory asks
adults to reflect honestly on past relationships, selfishness,
dishonesty, and harm done to others — using prompts drawn from the Big
Book, page 69.

The app itself contains no sexual depictions, no nudity, no explicit
language. The user types their own private reflections. The prompts are
therapeutic in nature and modeled directly on the paper workbook.

### 3. User-generated content shared with a specific partner

A user may type private inventory content and, optionally, share a
read-only snapshot with one specific person they have linked with — their
sponsor or sponsee. Users must both agree to the link by exchanging a
short code in person or by phone. There is no open chat, no public
feed, no messaging strangers.

---

## What the app does **not** contain

- No ads
- No third-party analytics or tracking
- No violence, depictions of violence, or imagery of self-harm
- No sexual content, imagery, or nudity
- No profanity
- No unrestricted web browsing
- No open social features, public profiles, or messaging strangers
- No gambling or contests
- No location tracking

---

## For parents and guardians

We do not recommend this app for anyone under 18. The content requires
adult context to be meaningful and could be upsetting or confusing
otherwise. If you are a parent with a child in a younger recovery program
(for example, Alateen), please look for age-appropriate resources designed
for that audience.

Apple's Screen Time parental controls can enforce the 18+ rating at the
device level.
